About Me

Passionate and driven, I am a Radiologic Technologist studying computer programming to improve patient and provider experiences.

I have been working healthcare for the past four years. I started as a front desk clerk and put myself through school to be a radiologic technologist. Working in medical imaging has been incredibly rewarding, however, I was introduced to coding by a friend who has worked as a full-stack engineer.

I am working to become a strong full-stack engineer to merge my love for healthcare with the innovative field of software engineering. I will be looking to work for a healthcare based company since I know what the needs of healthcare practioners as well as patients are.
